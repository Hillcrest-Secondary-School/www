Gr 8 & 9

The following subjects are compulsary for all learners:

  1. Home Language (Either English, Xhosa or Afrikaans)
  2. First Additional Language ( Either English or Afrikaans)
  3. Mathematics
  4. Natural Sciences
  5. Social Sciences (That consist of Geography and History)
  6. Technology
  7. Economic Management Sciences
  8. Life Orientation
  9. Creative Arts

Gr 10 - 12

  1. Home Language (Either English, Isixhosa or Afrikaans)
  2. First Additional Language ( Either English or Afrikaans)
  3. Life Orientation

For the remaining three subjects, any of the following combinations:

  1. Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, History
  2. Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Accounting
  3. Mathematics, Business Studies, Economics, Accounting
  4. Mathematical Literacy, Business Studies, Economics, Accounting
  5. Mathematical Literacy, Business Studies, Tourism, Geography
  6. Mathematical Literacy, Business studies, Tourism, History
  7. Mathematical Literacy, Consumer studies, Tourism, History
  8. Mathematical Literacy, Life Sciences, Tourism, History
