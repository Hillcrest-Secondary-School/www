Admission and Registration Policy of Hillcrest Secondary School

Policy Purpose

  • To meet the objectives of the Bill of Rights.
  • Support schools in developing admission policies within the prescripts of national and provincial legislation, including public and private laws.
  • Afford all children access to public schools within the prescripts of the law.
  • Facilitate the admission of learners in a timely and efficient manner.
  • Ensure the lawful administration of learner admission and registration.
  • Enable effective teaching and learning to commence on the first day of the school year and the first day of successive school terms.
  • And align the admission processes with the WCED CEMIS.

Admission

  • No charge fees such as registration fees, re-admission or preadmission testing fees, a deposit at the time of application.
  • The academic performance of learners may not determine admission.
  • Interviews with parents or learners should not be used for admission except for learners who wish to enter schools with specialist subjects.

Enrolment

Phase 1:

  • An application form for admission to the school is completed.
  • The school notifies the parent or learner that the application for admission has been successful or unsuccessful.

Phase 2:

  • The learner will be registered on the school CEMIS.

Admission Documents

  • Application form.
  • Official abridged birth certificate or id document.
  • Proof of immunisation.
  • Recent original school report.
  • An original school transfer letter from the school where the learner is enrolled.
  • Non South African citizens also need a temporary or permanent for residence or a study permit.

Registration Documents

  • All learners must be registered at the CEMIS system.
  • CEMIS Forms - Learner registration form.
  • CEMIS Transfer Certificate
  • CEMIS Deregistration Certificate
  • All information is written into the admission register.
  • Learner Profile

Online Registration - Parents can also use the online system to apply at the different schools.
